ABOUT IZUKU MIDORIYA

also known as Deku (デク, Deku?), is the main protagonist of the My Hero Academia manga and anime series

IZUKU'S CHARACTERISICS

  1. Izuku is a very timid, reserved, and polite boy, frequently overreacting to abnormal situations with exaggerated expressions
  2. Izuku is generally characterized as a nervous, honest, and cheerful boy. Despite being born Quirkless
  3. He's relatable, he's different from other Shonen Anime Protagonists, he isn't very confident
